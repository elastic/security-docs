=== WMI WBEMTEST Utility Execution

Adversaries may abuse the WMI diagnostic tool, wbemtest.exe, to enumerate WMI object instances or invoke methods against local or remote endpoints.

*Rule type*: eql

*Rule indices*: 

* logs-endpoint.events.*

*Severity*: low

*Risk score*: 21

*Runs every*: 60m

[source, js]
